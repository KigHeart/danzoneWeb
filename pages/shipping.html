Shipping Policy

Delivery times, costs and everything you need to know

Last updated: March 2026

Delivery Areas

DanaZone delivers to all counties in Kenya. We also ship internationally to select countries in East Africa including Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Ethiopia.

Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery times from the date of order confirmation:

  • Nairobi CBD & suburbs: Same day (orders before 12pm) or next business day
  • Greater Nairobi (within 30km): 1–2 business days
  • Major towns (Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret): 2–3 business days
  • Other Kenyan counties: 3–5 business days
  • International (EAC region): 5–10 business days

Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed on weekends are processed on the next business day.

Shipping Costs

  • FREE delivery on all orders over KES 2,000
  • Nairobi: KES 150 flat rate (orders under KES 2,000)
  • Within Kenya: KES 250–500 depending on location and weight
  • International: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation (Monday–Friday). You will receive an email and SMS confirmation with your order number once payment is verified, and a separate dispatch notification with tracking details when your order ships.

Order Tracking

Track your order at any time using the "Track Your Order" link in the header or footer, using your order number and registered email. You can also track via the courier's website using the tracking number provided in your dispatch email.

Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partners will attempt delivery up to 2 times. If delivery is unsuccessful, your item will be held at the nearest depot for 3 days before being returned to us. Re-delivery fees may apply. Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address or provide delivery instructions.

Damaged or Lost Items

If your order arrives damaged, please take photos immediately and contact us within 48 hours at support@danazone.co.ke. If your order is lost in transit, contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will investigate and arrange a replacement or refund.
