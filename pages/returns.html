Returns & Refunds

30-day hassle-free returns on most items

Last updated: March 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with your DanaZone purchase. If you're not happy, we make returns simple.

Return Window

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Electronics and appliances have a 14-day return window. Items must be in their original condition — unused, unwashed, with all original packaging, tags, manuals, and accessories.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, groceries)
  • Digital downloads and software licences
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Hazardous materials
  • Customised or personalised items
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Gift cards and vouchers

How to Return

  • Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Select the item(s) you wish to return and click "Request Return"
  • Select your reason for return and submit
  • You will receive a return authorisation email within 24 hours with instructions and a prepaid return label (for eligible returns)
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible
  • Drop off at any authorised DanaZone return point or arrange a pickup (KES 200 fee)

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • M-Pesa: Within 1–2 hours of approval
  • Card payments: 3–5 business days
  • PayPal: 1–3 business days
  • Store credit: Instant

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you receive a faulty, damaged, or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours at returns@danazone.co.ke with your order number and photos. We will arrange a free return and send a replacement or issue a full refund including original shipping costs.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To get a different size, colour, or product, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.
